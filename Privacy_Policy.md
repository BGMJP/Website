# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-18

This Privacy Policy describes how **ADCARRY Corp.** ("ADCARRY", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses and protects information processed through our Amazon reporting and analytics application (the "Application"). The Application is designed to generate weekly performance dashboards and reports for Amazon businesses.

By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you should not use the Application.

---

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- Data processed by the Application in connection with your Amazon operations, including advertising, inventory and customer service–related data.
- Data stored in generated reports and archives on local computers and/or cloud storage environments managed by ADCARRY or by you.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to any separate services or websites you access outside of the Application (including Amazon, Google or other third-party platforms), which are governed by their own privacy policies.

---

## 2. Information We Process

The Application is primarily a **data processing and reporting tool**. Depending on your configuration and data sources, the Application may process the following categories of information:

1. **Operational and Performance Data**
   - Advertising performance metrics (e.g., impressions, clicks, ad spend, ad sales, total sales, ACOS, TACoS, CTR, CVR).
   - Campaign, product and SKU-level performance data.

2. **Inventory and Logistics Data**
   - Inventory levels, inbound quantities, available units, restock recommendations.
   - FBA/FBM inventory data and Amazon Warehouse Dedicated–related fields.
   - Long-term storage exposure, days of supply and related metrics.

3. **Customer Service and Quality Data**
   - Seller feedback records (including ratings and free-text comments where provided in your data sources).
   - Voice of Customer (VoC) entries, including issue types, comments and related fields.
   - Product reviews, including review text, rating and related metadata.
   - Returns data for recent periods (e.g., 30-day returns), including SKU, reason codes and other operational fields.

4. **Identifiers from Your Data Sources**
   - Amazon-specific identifiers such as ASIN, SKU, Merchant SKU, Order ID and similar fields.
   - Account or company identifiers and country information as provided in your data exports.

The Application is **not intended** to collect or process direct customer PII such as full customer names, physical addresses, phone numbers or payment card data. To the extent such information appears in your underlying exports, you are responsible for ensuring that your configuration and use comply with applicable data protection laws and Amazon policies.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information processed by the Application solely for the following purposes:

1. **Reporting and Analytics**  
   - To generate weekly dashboards, HTML reports and tables that summarize your Amazon business performance across ads, inventory and customer service.
   - To produce internal dashboards for your team or your clients, including per-brand and per-country comparisons.

2. **Maintenance and Improvement**  
   - To monitor the performance and reliability of the Application.
   - To troubleshoot issues, improve data normalization and enhance visualizations and metrics.

3. **Compliance and Protection**  
   - To comply with applicable laws, regulations and Amazon policies.
   - To protect the rights, property and safety of ADCARRY, our users and others.

We do **not** use your data for unrelated advertising, resale or profiling outside the scope of providing and improving the Application.

   - You may delete or overwrite local copies and archives in your environment at any time, subject to your own internal policies and legal obligations.
